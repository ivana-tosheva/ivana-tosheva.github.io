# Nutrition for Beginners

Good nutrition is essential for improving fitness and staying healthy.  
Eating balanced meals helps your body recover and gain strength.

## Basic Nutrition Tips

- Drink enough water every day
- Eat foods rich in protein
- Include vegetables in every meal
- Avoid too much sugar and processed food

## Healthy Foods for Fitness

### Protein
Protein helps build and repair muscles.

Examples:
- Chicken
- Eggs
- Greek yogurt
- Fish

### Healthy Carbohydrates
Carbs give your body energy for workouts.

Examples:
- Oatmeal
- Brown rice
- Whole grain bread
- Potatoes

### Hydration
Drink at least **1.5–2 liters of water** per day.
